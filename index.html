About Me

Hi! I'm Samrat, User Experience and Interaction Designer from Georgia Institute of Technology with
5+ years of experience in Interaction Design, Wireframing, Rapid Prototyping and Usability Testing.
I have a background in computer science that, coupled with design skills, gives me the ability to
conceptualize, design and build interactive applications. I believe that every day provides us with opportunities
to contemplate upon, to design and to learn from our surroundings. But sometimes it takes an open mind,
a childish curiosity and the courage to do things the wrong way that builds new experiences.
