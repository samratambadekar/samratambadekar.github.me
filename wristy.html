Wristy: A Wearable Smartwatch Concept


Goal

The goal was to design a portable timekeeping device. The tools used are pen and markers.

The Process

First the physical properties of the product were decided which would help in guiding the design. These were:

  • • The material will be Glass (primary) and chrome metal (secondary).
  • • The color would be Transparent (primary) and Red (secondary).
  • • The texture would be smooth.
  • • Sound would be Beep (primary) and Dong (secondary)

Moodboard


Three words used to envision the design: Clear - Visible - Elegance.

A moodboard was designed using these words.
